How To Read It

This view is meant to help you decide which drafting style deserves manual polish.

  1. The rule-based draft gives you the stable ATS-aware baseline.
  2. The LLM draft adds tone, employer framing, and more expressive evidence selection.
  3. Use the notes and text side by side to decide which parts to keep, merge, or rewrite.
